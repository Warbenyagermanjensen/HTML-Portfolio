Best Movies of All Time According to James

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Predator

Great movie with nostalgic graphics that still holds up today. A bit campy, but not too much.

Toy Story

One of the best all-around movies ever made. I have great memories enjoying it as a kid, as well as memories watching it with my boys on the couch while we were all at home with COVID.

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Technically 3 movies, but I'll interpret it as 1 for this because it's all one story. The "Star Wars" of my generation, though infinitely better than any of the Star Wars movies.
